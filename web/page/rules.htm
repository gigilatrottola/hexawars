Rules

Hexawars is a turn-based multiplayer strategy game.

The goal of the game is to occupy the forts with your armies. At the beginning every player has the same number of forts, with an army in each. There are empty forts on the rest of the game area.

Every turn lasts a limited time. The players make their move simultaneously. You can determine the movement of your armies for more turns in advance.

You can occupy a fort with your army if you stop on it or pass trough it. If the fort was empty, it will be yours without a battle.

If an army meets an enemy army, the battle takes place automatically.

The players get reinforcements every turn automatically in the occupied forts.

The game ends if one of the players occupies all of the forts, or the last turn is over. in this case, the player with the most occupied forts wins, if the number of occupied forts is equal, the player with more soldiers wins.

Scoring
In a three players game the winner gets 3 points the second gets 1 points and the looser 0.
In the two players game the winner gets 2 points the looser gets 0 points.
In the beginner games the opponents don't get points for the victory.
